Varick Classics Vol. 1
About the composer & song index
🎤
Dr. James Varrick Armaah
Composer · Conductor · PhD Music, Univ. of Ghana (Feb 2026)
Born in Winneba, Ghana (27 April 1984), Dr. Armaah began composing at age seven and founded Harmonious Chorale Ghana in 2005, now one of Africa’s foremost interdenominational choirs. He has composed over 63 gospel songs across five collections — the Varick Classics series. His signature piece “Oye” gained global fame, performed by the Chinese police band during a Ghanaian presidential state visit in 2018. He also leads Varick Chorus UK, his London-based choir.
🎉 Varick Classics Vol. 5 launched 18 Apr 2026 📍 Upper Tooting Methodist Church, London

About This Book

Varick Classics Vol. 1 is a collection of 20 songs arranged for SATB choral groups with piano accompaniment — spanning passion & resurrection anthems, general songs, highlife pieces, and one English anthem.

“Bless the Lord, O my soul and all that is within me bless his holy name.” — Psalm 103:1  ·  James Varrick Armaah

About the Choir

The GMF Chaplaincy Choir is the music ministry of the Ghanaian Methodist Fellowship UK & Ireland. We minister through hymns, anthems, highlife, patriotic and classical choral music to the glory of God. This is a Ghanaian Methodist Church Choir based in London.
